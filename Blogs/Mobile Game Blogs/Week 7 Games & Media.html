How To Make Our Game Jucier

Our Lecturer Hope put on a very useful video in class on how to make our game much better. The video was 2 guys who were showcasing a simple game of breakout at first but then they kept progressively adding new features to the game to make it a lot more fun. They added things such as particle effects for when the ball collides with something, screen shake effect for when the ball breaks a block, adding sound effects and a bunch of other features that turned a simple boring game of breakout into a fun game with more life and personality.

This video gave me and my teammates a lot of ideas on how to make our game more exciting such as:

  • Trails that follow the items
  • Reminding us what kind of sound effects we should add to the game and how they should be used
  • Background music that fits the game
