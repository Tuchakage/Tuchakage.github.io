Week 4 - Cinematics Week

Date Created: Feb 27, 2022

We had a guest speaker today to come in to talk about cinematics in Unreal Engine. He gave us a good presentations on how to do Cinematics in Unreal Engine and then after he came round to me to give me a one to one session on how to get started with Cinematics.

He taught me how to create the level sequences, how to animate stuff for the cutscene and by the end of it i was able to create one of the panels in our story board which has now been scrapped because our narrative designer wants to make a better one.
