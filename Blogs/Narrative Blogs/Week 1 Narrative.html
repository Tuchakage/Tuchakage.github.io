Week 1 - New Project, New Team!

Date Created: Feb 4, 2022

This week we started the new project given to us, for this project we have to create a story based game. For this project we stuck with the same team that we had for our mobile game "Donut Pack" but we added in 3 new members in Andrew, David and Alfred.

After creating the teams we needed a way where we could come up with ideas for our game, thats when i got started on creating a Trello and created a section where members of our team could add in story ideas and a section where we can talk about the characters in our game. I also created a card that helps us how we should plan out the story line, and a card that contains things on what we should be thinking about when creating a character.
