Week 13 - Adding A Sword

Date Created: Apr 4, 2022

Adding A Two Handed Sword

The bow and Dagger weapons are cool weapon ideas but I felt like I should at least add one more weapon that was stronger than the rest. Not only will the sword weapon be stronger it will also have a blocking feature, the one that is currently on the dagger to be exact.

For the Sword i just duplicated the DaggerBP and renamed it SwordBP and then i changed the mesh to be of a Sword and gave the Sword the tag "Blockable" so that the Enemy knows it has collided with a weapon that can block.

Second Hand Grabbing Dilema

Similar to the bow i've made it so that the Sword can be grabbed with a second hand but when the sword is grabbed with the second hand the sword will rotate towards the 2nd hand like it would real life, this is done with some calculations in the Event Tick that will keep tracking the second hand (See "Second Hand Tracking Before" image below)

The only problem with the 2nd hand tracking for the Sword is that it was very uncomfortable for the player to hold it so i scrapped that idea for a much more simpler idea where when the player grabs the sword with their second hand then it will just rotate the sword on the Y and Z axis.
