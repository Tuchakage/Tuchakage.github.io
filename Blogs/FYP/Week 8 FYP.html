Date Created: Feb 18, 2022

Adding Full Body To The Main Project!

I was able to figure out the reason why the body would just teleport to the VR origin instead of smoothly follow it, the reason being is that the if statement that makes the player model automatically follow the VR origin is only called when the movement threshold of the player in real life is over a certain amount. To fix this solution I just got rid of that if statement and now the Player model will move with the VR origin.

After figuring that out it took me a while to add it into the main project because of all the functions it requires and i had to import over all the animations and things like that whilst also cleaning up the Blueprints so it is more readable.

After i had finished adding in the basics of full body into the main project i noticed how the arms of the player model was really short and that could cause a problem later on for the Bow & Arrow, but before i fixed that problem i wanted to see if i can make it so that the Player can pick up the weapons with its new player model.
