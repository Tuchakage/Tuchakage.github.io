Date Created: Mar 27, 2022

Grabbing Animation

Thanks to the grab anim montage that i had created before i was able to sucessfully make it so that the grabbing animation will play depending on the trigger you press on your VR controller, meaning that if you pressed the left trigger on your left controller the left grab animation will play and for the right controller the right hand would play.

I had to use cache poses once again to make each individual finger rotate whenever the player presses the trigger, before doing this method i tried to do the easier way where i just played the anim montage for the whole hand but that just resulted in the hand being rotated like it is in the anim montage.

Since you can use a branch node with the cached pose in order to separate the left and right hands grab animation i had to control the "Blend Weights 0" value on the Layered Blend per bone node by creating a variable that would control this float variable. When you press the trigger for the left hand then it sets that variable to 1 and when its released it sets it to 0, the same for the right hand (Look at "Playing Left Grab Anim" image below).
