Week 2 - Making Our Dagger Weapon Unique

Date Created: Nov 10, 2021

Small Update To The Game

Since i had the C++ Weapon component in the elongated cube Blueprint i decided that i would just make that the Dagger and just replace the Mesh later on. For the Dagger i wanted to make it have an ability where you are able to teleport to your Dagger, i got the inspiration from characters such as Minato from the show Naruto and Noctis from the game Final Fantasy 15 where they have a really cool ability where they can teleport to anywhere there weapon is.

I then decided to reduce the speed of when you rotate your camera because it was going way too fast and players could become motion sick.

Small Update To The Game

I then decided that i should quickly add in a bow and arrow since i realised it would be nothing like doing the Melee weapons. I found a tutorial on Youtube which required me to install a plugin that expands the things i can do on the Unreal Engine for VR.

I watched the tutorial first to make sure i understand what im doing and then on another day i would go ahead and actually implement the things, before closing the project i quickly imported the Bow and Arrow assets given by the video and i added the VR slider that comes with the VR expansion plugin.
