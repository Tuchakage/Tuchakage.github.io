The Last Arena VR (So Far)


Sneak Peek At Combat


Testing Out Full Body

Solo Project
Game Engine: Unreal Engine 4
Platform: VR

The Last Arena VR is a VR action game where you will be put into an arena to fight waves of enemies.

In The Last Arena VR you will have a variety of different weapons that will help you survive the waves of enemies and they will give you a unique way of fighting.

Progress So far

• Implemented VR Combat so Enemy and the player can deal damage to each other

• Added a dagger weapon (Mesh for now is an elongated cube) that does damage and has a special ability where you can teleport to it, it also has knockback

• Added a Bow and Arrow that does damage and has knockback

• Player can grab weapons

• Added Enemy AI that uses a Behaviour Tree to follow and attack the Enemy

• Player can block the Enemies attacks with certain weapons

• Got full body immersion to work on a separate project but now just need to adjust the height of the camera and need to make it so the player character has a grab animation
