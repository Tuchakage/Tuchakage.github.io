OpenGL Environment

Solo Project
Made With: Open GL GLSL Shaders
Platform: PC

In This Project i am using what i have learnt to create an environment on Open GL

My Contribution:

Since this was a Solo Project i did everything myself.

Controls:

WSAD – Movement

Q – Fly Up

E – Fly Down

Click & Hold Left Or Mouse Button – Look Around

T – Toggle Attenuation

N – Toggle Day & Night

Y – Toggle Weather

U – Toggle Special Effects

Different Effects:

  • Press 1 for INSTAGRAM-STYLE SEPIA FILTER

  • Press 2 for BLUR FILTER

  • Press 3 for KERNEL EFFECT

  • Press 4 for EDGE DETECTION FILTER

  • Press 5 for NIGHT VISION GOGGLES

  • Press 6 for FISH EYE DISTORTION EFFECT
