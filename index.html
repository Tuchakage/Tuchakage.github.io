About Me!

I’m Tuchan Anthony, currently a Senior Games Technician at Kingston University who loves to program games. Before getting the Games Technician role I was an Undergraduate student in the Games Programming course where I got through with a 1st Class Honour.

I would class myself as someone who loves to learn new things and improving on my skills, which is one of the reasons why for my Final Year Project for my Undergraduate course I decided to create a VR gladiator game called “The Last Arena”.

I haven’t had much experience with Unreal Engine at this point and have only used it for one project prior, I have also never created a VR game but I wanted to test myself and see how well I could do with an engine that is known to be difficult to learn with a genre of games that University professors don’t see much from students as a final year project, in the end I would say it went really well and I was extremely proud of myself!
